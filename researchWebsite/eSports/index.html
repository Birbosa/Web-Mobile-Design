E-Sports

Esports vs Casual Competition

Often seen today are Esport tournaments in games such as Leauge of Legends, Counter Strike 2, and Valorant that bring in a ton of fans, giant arenas, and massive prize pools that would bring other events to shame. On the other hand however, games like team fortress 2 have a much lesser seen, but more casual approach to both esports and gaming as a whole. Instead of trying to be the next hit thing like Valorant or LoL, it instead focuses on what makes a game enjoyable and bridges the gap between playing to win, and playing for fun.

The future of esports


Esports today are a much different from what the were just a few years ago. what once was a way to get views on youtube, or recognized as a kids dream job, has now become a way to fame and riches. As a result however, the fun and charm of the industry has instead been switched out for a way to market products, games, and bring in views from all around the world. In terms of how the future will go, it is widely unknown, with the only definite fact being the continuation of the cultural phenomenon being guranteed.
