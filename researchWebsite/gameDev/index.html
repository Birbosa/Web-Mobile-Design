Game Dev

Unity Engine

- Some games made with unity include: hollow knight, among us, lethal company, slay the spire, and cult of the lamb to name a few
- The program is a great introduction to game creation and allows the browsing and use of pre-made assets in games
- The downside is engine limitations and the new fees imposed by the company

Unreal Engine


- Unreal is an engine used in both game creation as well as other productions
- Popular games include: fortnight, payday 3, and ark survival evolved
- Unreal requires much more experience than unity, however the limitations on creation is much smaller as a result
